Adesh Grewal Engineering Portfolio

Hi everyone, I am Adesh an enthusiatic roboitcs engineer who loves tinkering with technology and have always been intrigued by mechanisms and the working of things wether it be a toy in my childhood or a decision making algorithm. Here I am Showcasing my robotics and engineering projects that i have done over the years

Assistive Robotics — Spot Integration

Developed a 3-DOF robotic arm integrated with Boston Dynamics Spot robot for assistive guidance in collaboration with Guide Dogs Australia Association. Used capstan-tendon actuation for lightweight, compliant human-robot interaction. Led full system design including SolidWorks + FEA, workspace optimization in MATLAB, URDF modeling, and PID tuning in ROS2. Conducted HIL testing validating reachability, safety, and back-drivability.

UTS Motorsports Autonomous Race Car

Led an 8-member mechanical team to convert a race car for autonomous operation. Designed a dual-mode pedal box and Ackermann steering mechanism, achieving sub-3s system latency. Closely collaborated with electrical and software teams for integration of the full autonomous stack.

Vision-Based Manipulation with Robot Arm

Programmed a robot arm for image-guided pick-and-place tasks using eye-to-hand calibration. Integrated RGBD sensors, tag detection, and OpenCV-based visual servoing to enable real-time feedback and precision grasping.

Space Robotics — Autonomous Navigation

Implemented A*, Dijkstra, PRM, and frontier exploration algorithms for planetary terrain simulations. Integrated YOLOv11 with LIDAR and RGBD data for perception, fusing LIDAR, IMU, and camera data to achieve robust autonomous navigation and object detection in Gazebo using ROS1 and Python.

Multi-Robot Collaboration and Land Surveying

Developed C++ ROS2 control stacks for quadcopter, Ackermann, and skid-steer robots. Implemented threading for efficient multi-agent collaboration and resource management in distributed robotic systems.

Acoustic Materials Research

Conducted laser vibrometer experiments on damping materials, performing mode shape analysis in Sim Centre. Investigated structural response and tested materials for acoustic insulation at UTS Botany Bay.

Shot Blasting Process Optimization

Improved shot-blasting efficiency at Mahindra & Mahindra by analyzing consumable usage and operational parameters. Contributed to reducing process cost and enhancing surface finish consistency.

3D-Printed RF Sensors

Designed 3D-printed RF sensors using recycled plastic blends during an internship at NTU & Panjab University. Investigated dielectric property variations and their effects on antenna gain. Published results in Springer Journal (link).

Autonomous Delta-Wing UAV

Designed and fabricated an autonomous fixed delta-wing UAV for aerial surveillance. Integrated GPS waypoint tracking and gyro-stabilized flight control for robust autonomous missions.

National Electric Kart Championship (NEKC 3.0)

Led the mechanical design for the winning team, focusing on ergonomics and highly responsive steering systems. Successfully competed and secured top national rank.

Auto India Racing Championship (AIRC)

Contributed to chassis design and steering optimization for the race car. Team placed 16th nationally, demonstrating improvements in handling and structural integrity.
